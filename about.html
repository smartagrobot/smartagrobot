Our Mission

Agrobot Technologies was founded with a singular vision: to make sustainable farming accessible and efficient through automation.

We believe that the future of agriculture lies not in working harder, but in working smarter. By delegating repetitive and physically demanding tasks to intelligent machines, we empower farmers to focus on what truly matters—growing high-quality, healthy crops.

HARDWARE & SOFTWARE

The Tech Behind The Bot

Agrobot is built on robust, industry-standard hardware and cutting-edge software.

Microcontrollers

Powered by ESP32 and Arduino for reliable, low-latency control logic.

Computer Vision

ESP32-CAM modules running trained ML models to distinguish weeds from crops.

Precision Mechanics

Custom stepper motor drivers for exact seed spacing and navigational accuracy.

Cloud Connectivity

Real-time data synchronization with the Blynk IoT dashboard for remote management.
