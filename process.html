Autonomous Cycle

A step-by-step breakdown of the autonomous agricultural workflow, from computer vision analysis to cloud-based monitoring.

01

Smart Weeding

The process begins with vision. Agrobot utilizes an ESP-32 CAM module equipped with a trained machine learning model.

  • • Scans the field row-by-row.
  • • Differentiates between crop leaves and weeds.
  • • Activates high-speed cutter fan on detection.

Seed Dispensing

Precision planting is key. Agrobot employs a custom-designed hopper mechanism controlled by a stepper motor.

Interval: Every 30cm

Prevents overcrowding & ensures nutrients.

02
03

Moisture Control

Agrobot inserts capacitive soil moisture sensors into the ground at regular checkpoints to prevent over/under-watering.

LOGIC FLOW

If Moisture < 30% → Trigger Alert & Water

If Moisture > 80% → Stop Watering

Water Dispensing

Based on sensor data, the onboard pump activates. The robot utilizes a localized drip-style system.

Reduces Usage by 40%
